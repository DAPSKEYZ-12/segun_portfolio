About Me

Building Confident
Mathematicians

I am Oluwasegun Micheal Fasika, a dedicated and passionate Mathematics Educator based in Asaba, Delta State, Nigeria, with over five years of proven experience transforming students who struggle with numbers into confident, high-performing learners.

My approach blends structured pedagogy with empathy; I meet every learner where they are and guide them systematically to where they need to be. Whether you are a Primary school pupil building foundational skills or a Secondary school student preparing for a high-stakes examination, I design every session around your unique learning style and pace.

I specialise in international and national examination preparation including SAT, IGCSE, WAEC, GCE and NECO. My special intervention programme is specifically crafted for learners who have struggled with Mathematics and need a fresh, patient, and effective approach to unlock their potential.
